UPDATES FROM THE SHEPHERDS REST TEAM:

Latest Update:

5th April 2021

As promised, we are now able to announce that The Sheps will reopen on Tuesday 4th May. As long as the governments roadmap out of lockdown stays to plan, we will be able to welcome you indoors from Monday 17th May, with this in mind, please note that going indoors until this time, will only be possible to access the toilets. We hope the British weather is kind and you can enjoy The Sheps new “Secret Garden”, a space we have reclaimed back from nature during lockdown. If you wish to book a table for your group (up to 6 people) please give us a call on 01823 339602. It has been a long time and I speak for all the team when I say “We cannot wait to see you all soon”. You lovely people make “The Sheps” what it is and without you here it’s been a very sad time indeed. Here’s to the future and the return of “Good Food, Good Ale & Good Times”

Take care of yourselves, be healthy and be happy

Covid-19 Update:

30th November 2020

December is just around the corner and with all preparations in place, we were getting ready to open our doors again and welcome you all back to The Sheps next week for what is normally the busiest month of the year.

However, with TIER 2 restrictions being so tight within our industry and after giving the subject much agonizing thought over the past couple of days (as to not only how it will affect us but your experience of visiting) it is disheartening to let you all know that unfortunately the doors will be remaining closed until the 16th December. After which we hope for a better outcome of the tiering system.

Christmas at The Shepherds Rest:

December 2020

Ok, so its not been the best year and its certainly one that will go down in history as pretty unforgettable for many reasons.
Here at ‘The Sheps’ we want to make sure we all have a well deserved festive period whilst keeping to current rules/guidance regarding the COVID 19 pandemic.
